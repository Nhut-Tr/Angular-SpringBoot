About us

Dinh Nhut Construction and Trade Investment Joint Stock Company owns DinhNhut2K Mobile store chain - a leading retailer, specializing in providing genuine technology products in the Vietnamese market. In 1996, DinhNhut2K Mobile was established, gradually becoming a reliable address of Vietnamese consumers. With the slogan "If what we don't have, you don't need it", we have been and will continue to strive to bring a wide variety of genuine technology products with the best prices. serve the needs of your customers. After more than 20 years of development, DinhNhut2K Mobile has become a name that is no longer strange to domestic consumers. Currently, we own a network of more than 100 branches covering the whole country, including two service centers in Hanoi and one service center in Ho Chi Minh City. Coming to DinhNhut2K Mobile's store chain, you can be completely assured of the prestige and quality of products with prices about 15-20% cheaper than the market price. Along with that, we also always strive to serve to bring the best service experience to our customers.

    Important milestones:


  • 1996: Established the first DinhNhut2K Mobile store located on the busy street of Hanoi capital at 194 Le Duan.
  • 2000: DinhNhut2K Mobile officially became a genuine mobile phone distributor in cooperation with many leading brands such as Samsung, OPPO, Nokia, Huawei,...
  • 2006: With 10 years of experience in retail, DinhNhut2K Mobile developed strongly and continuously opened many new branches.
  • 2016: Celebrating 20 years of operation in the field of retail technology products, Hoang Ha Mobile has affirmed a solid foothold in the market as well as in the subconscious of consumers.
  • In 2019: DinhNhut2K Mobile officially cooperated with the telecom giant MobiFone Vietnam to open a chain of affiliate sales branches, bringing the total number of branches to more than 60, and covering 30 provinces and cities across the country. Since then, DinhNhut2K Mobile has continuously accompanied MobiFone to organize large and small events.
  • 2020: DinhNhut2K Mobile is proud to become the official authorized retailer of Apple in Vietnam. Genuine Apple VN/A products distributed by Apple Vietnam are sold at Hoang Ha Mobile system with the best market price and prestigious warranty.
